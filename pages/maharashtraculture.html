FOOD & CUISINE

The spicy, tangy and iconic flavors of Maharashtra

STREET FOOD

Vada Pav

Mumbai's popular and beloved spicy potato fritter placed inside a bread bun served with dry garlic chutney.

SPICY BREAKFAST

Misal Pav

A spicy curry made of sprouted moth beans topped with crispy farsan, raw onions and served with buttery pav.

SWEET DESSERT

Puran Poli

A festive sweet flatbread stuffed with a smooth and rich filling of yellow lentils, jaggery and cardamom.

MUMBAI SPECIAL

Pav Bhaji

A thick vegetable curry prepared in butter, served hot with soft bread rolls and chopped raw onions.

FESTIVE DESSERT

Ukdiche Modak

Sweet steamed rice-flour dumplings stuffed with fresh grated coconut and sweet jaggery.

CULTURE GLIMPSES

Explore dance, food, craft & festivals of Maharashtra

DANCE

Lavani Folk Dance

A energetic, traditional dance performed to the rhythmic beats of Dholki, famous for its cultural charm.

CUISINE

Traditional Flavors

A rich balance of coastal coconut milk spices and rustic dry peanut masalas from the mainland.

CRAFT

Warli Art

A tribal art style using geometric shapes to depict daily life, nature, and celebrations on mud walls.

HERITAGE

Maratha Fortresses

Strong hilltop and sea forts constructed by Shivaji Maharaj, displaying outstanding defense engineering.

FESTIVAL

Ganesh Chaturthi

The grandest festival of the state, celebrating Lord Ganesha with massive processions, music, and prayers.

TEXTILE

Paithani Silk Weaving

Beautiful hand-woven silk sarees containing peacock motifs on zari borders, representing Maratha royalty.

COMMUNITY

Koli Fishermen Culture

The original inhabitants of Mumbai's coast, famous for lively dances, songs, and seafood cuisine.
