FOOD & CUISINE

The rich, aromatic and royal flavors of Rajasthan

DAL BAATI CHURMA

Dal Baati Churma

Baked round wheat flour dough balls served with rich spiced lentils and sweet ghee-loaded churma.

SPICY CURRY

Laal Maas

A fiery and legendary mutton curry cooked in yogurt and spiced hot Mathania red chilies.

SAVORY SNACK

Pyaaz Kachori

Deep-fried crisp pastry stuffed with a highly spiced onion filling, served with sweet tamarind chutney.

FESTIVE SWEET

Rajasthani Ghewar

A traditional disc-shaped honeycomb sweet cake made with flour and ghee, soaked in sugar syrup.

STREET SNACK

Mirchi Vada

Spicy chili fritters stuffed with mashed potato filling, coated in gram flour batter and deep fried.
