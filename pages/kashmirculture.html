FOOD & CUISINE

The warm, aromatic and royal spices of Kashmir

ROYAL FEAST

Kashmiri Wazwan

A multi-course formal meal featuring tender lamb recipes cooked with Kashmiri red chilies and rich spices.

SIGNATURE DISH

Rogan Josh

A Persian-influenced slow-cooked lamb curry colored naturally with alkanet root (ratan jot) and mild spices.

TRADITIONAL TEA

Kashmiri Kahwa

Exotic green tea brewed with saffron strands, crushed cinnamon, green cardamom, and slivered almonds.

VEGETARIAN DELIGHT

Dum Aloo

Deep-fried baby potatoes slow-cooked in a spicy yogurt and fennel-flavored Kashmiri red chili gravy.

MINCED MEATBALLS

Gushtaba

Spiced minced mutton meatballs cooked in a velvety, flavorful yogurt-based gravy (Yakhni).

Shikara Riding
