About Gujarat

Gujarat is famous for its vibrant festivals, Garba dance, rich handicrafts, spiritual heritage, and warm hospitality. It reflects simplicity, discipline, and joyful traditions.

Did you know?

Capital: Gandhinagar
Birthplace of Mahatma Gandhi
Home to the Asiatic Lion in Gir Forest
Famous for Garba, Bandhani textiles, and White Desert of Kutch
Major Language: Gujarati

Folk & Tradition

Gujarat’s folk and traditions are vibrant, joyful, and deeply rooted in community life. Folk dances like **Garba** and **Dandiya Raas** celebrate devotion and togetherness, especially during Navratri. Traditional music, colorful attire, handicrafts, and festivals reflect simplicity, discipline, and cultural pride. Through its customs and celebrations, Gujarat preserves a rich heritage that blends spirituality with everyday life.
