FOOD & CUISINE

The sweet, savory and globally-loved Gujarati flavors

GUJARATI SNACKS

Dhokla & Thepla

Steamed savory chickpea-flour Khaman Dhoklas, and flatbreads made with fenugreek leaves (Thepla).

SUNDAY SPECIAL

Fafda Jalebi

A classic combination of crispy chickpea flour crackers (Fafda) paired with sweet syrup-filled Jalebis.

WINTER DISH

Undhiyu

A rich mixed vegetable casserole prepared with local winter roots, baby eggplants, and fenugreek dumplings.

SAVORY ROLL

Khandvi

Soft, tightly rolled savory snacks made from chickpea flour paste cooked with yogurt and spiced mustard oil.

SWEET & SOUR

Gujarati Kadhi

A sweet and tangy yogurt-based soup cooked with gram flour, green chilies, ginger, and aromatic tempering.

CULTURE GLIMPSES

Explore dance, food, craft & heritage of Gujarat

DANCE

Garba & Dandiya

Folk dances performed in circles during the nine nights of the Navratri festival.

CUISINE

Traditional Flavors

Flavorful sweet & savory vegetarian meals served with fried green chilies and chutneys.

LANDSCAPE

Rann Desert Safari

Camel safaris across the vast white desert sands of Kutch under moonlit skies.

HERITAGE

Intricate Monuments

Stepwells, step towers and carvings representing ancient Indian structural design.

FESTIVAL

Navratri Lights

A high-energy, colorful nine-night festival filled with devotional songs, traditional dress, and community.

FESTIVAL

Uttarayan (Kite Festival)

The International Kite Festival where millions of colorful kites fill the blue skies of Gujarat in January.

WILDLIFE

Gir Asiatic Lions

The exclusive sanctuary preserving the majestic Asiatic Lion population in the dry deciduous Gir forest.
