FOOD

Vadapav | MUMBAI

A popular Mumbai street food made of a spicy potato fritter placed inside a bread bun with chutneys.

Chole bhature | DELHI

A famous North Indian dish of spicy chickpea curry served with deep-fried fluffy bread

Dosa | CHENNAI

A thin, crispy South Indian pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter, usually served with chutney and sambar.

Rosogolla | KOLKATA

A soft and spongy sweet made from chhena (paneer) balls soaked in sugar syrup, popular in Bengal.

Biryani | HYDERABAD

A flavorful rice dish cooked with aromatic spices, rice, and vegetables or meat, widely loved across India.

Dal Bhati | JAIPUR

A traditional Rajasthani dish consisting of baked wheat balls (baati), lentil curry (dal), and sweet crushed churma.
